Our purpose

At Edward Jones, we are fueled by our purpose — to partner for positive impact, to improve the lives of our clients and colleagues, and better our communities and society. This purpose informs our actions and decisions. It's present in the work our branch teams do each day, building deep, personal relationships with our clients and delivering comprehensive financial planning and advice.

As a firm that seeks to make a positive difference in the world, we address some of the most pressing challenges of our time. We call these our Purpose Impact Areas: partnering for lasting financial strength, promoting healthier futures and advancing inclusive growth.

Partnering for lasting financial strength

We believe in the power of financial knowledge and confidence and a personal, needs-based approach to build long-lasting financial strength.

In 2025, $6.5 million awarded to students for higher education and $2.9 million to small businesses.
Promoting healthier futures

We activate our purpose to support the financial, physical and emotional well-being of those impacted by health issues.

Since 2016, over $58 million raised for the Alzheimer's Association, with 134,000+ participants in the Walk to End Alzheimer's.
Advancing inclusive growth

We invest to lift all people and communities, including the underserved, by equipping more people to fully participate in the economy.

Edward Jones supported nearly 161,000 community jobs in the U.S., translating into $21.5 billion of labor income — together contributing $29.5 billion to the U.S. GDP.*
*Source: Edward Jones, Purpose, Inclusion, & Citizenship Report: Finding Fulfillment, 3/17/2026.
